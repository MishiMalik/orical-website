Orical Partners are experts in investment adviser and broker/dealer compliance programs. Our Managing Partners, Greg Florio and Jim Leahy, joined forces while working together at a multi-billion-dollar global fund manager, as Senior Counsel/Chief Compliance Officer and Chief Financial Officer, respectively. They came up with the idea to create a new type of consulting service and software company focused on helping clients grow and succeed by implementing institutional quality compliance and operational programs for every type of client in a cost-effective and business-savvy way.
